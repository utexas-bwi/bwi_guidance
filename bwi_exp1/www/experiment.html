Controls:
Move Forward = W
Move Backward = S
Strafe Left = A
Strage Right = D
Turn Left = ← OR Move Mouse Left (Hit the "Use Mouse!" button to use the mouse)
Turn Right = → OR Move Mouse Right (Hit the "Use Mouse!" button to use the mouse)

Enter = Continue to next experiment
ESC = Get Mouse Back
P = Pause

Tips:
If you stop moving, the image stream to your browser may have broken. Hit refresh to reconnect to the image stream. This can also happen if you are too close to a wall, so try backing away from the wall.
